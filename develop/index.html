Studio Masterclass

Dual-Stream Workflow

Import your massive RAW exports. Seaside creates 800px Smart Previews for fluid dashboard performance. When you "Bake," the engine compresses originals to 2048px (<400KB) for the perfect balance of quality and speed.

Typography & Design

Choose separate Google Fonts for Titles and Body. Use the Tracking and Line Height sliders to match your brand's editorial style. Use the Reset button next to each slider to return to the professional baseline.

Interactive Content

Select any text in the header to trigger the Floating Toolbar. Apply bolding, underlining, or insert live links to client websites or social media. Delete session images instantly using the trash icon on hover.

Baking & Deployment

The "Bake" process generates a structured project ZIP. Extract the contents and upload them directly to GitHub Pages, AWS, or your own hosting for a white-labeled client experience.
